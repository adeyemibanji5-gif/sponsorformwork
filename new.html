DIFFERENT NIGERIAN DISHES

Nigerian cuisine features diverse dishes, including flavorful soups like Egusi, Ogbono, and Afang,
often served with swallow (fufu, pounded yam, eba). Staple dishes include Jollof Rice, various stews,
and dishes made from beans, like Akara (bean balls) and Moi Moi (steamed bean pudding).

TYPES OF DIFFERENT NIGERIAN DISHES

Jollof Rice: A vibrant, flavorful rice dish cooked with tomatoes, peppers, and various spices.

Fried Rice: Another popular rice dish, often made with vegetables and meat or fish.

Ofada Rice: Unpolished local rice served with a flavorful Ofada stew.

Coconut Rice: A rice dish made with coconut milk for a rich, sweet flavor.

Pounded Yam: A dense, starchy food made from pounded yam.
